Welcome


B.V Jagadeesh Science Centre is Jayanagar's complex for museum, library, independent research and education. We are a community open for learners, explorers and inventors in STEM. Join us on a journey of knowledge discovery.


Visit

The BVJ Science Centre's Museum, Library, and Movie hall are open to the public for Free. Fix an appointment with us to obtain your entry passes. Our facilities remain temporarily closed.

Even when we are closed, you can visit us from home. Explore our digital resources including online events, and discover how we care to communicate science.
